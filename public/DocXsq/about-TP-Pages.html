The pages in DocXsq are typical for KW process of managing information
    for IT and everything else.

Principles-
    Pages are quickly editable
    Chunks are kept small to visually fit on a medium laptop screen, usually
    Scrolling is to be avoided- Eyes move much faster than the fingers.
    Links connect concepts-
        association of similar elements
        zoom links  - to more detail or less (more an overview)

BrowZedit  - The holy grail is to be able to Browse pages quickly and
        shift to edit mode in one gesture- ctrl/E or swipe
                WYSIWYG edit and exit  (always save ! )
        creating new pages - also quickly, effective copy & paste
                Rapidly create good links among pages

Identify Overview pages - Lists, Table of Contents, Index of terms/glossary

KKeys - designated keywords - actually tags - in subtitle line by convention inside {  }
